About the System

A smart safety solution for homes and kitchens

The project aims to reduce the risk of gas explosions by combining low-cost sensing technology with connected monitoring.

Project Background

LPG is a common fuel for cooking, but leakages can occur silently and lead to fires or explosions. The proposed system uses an MQ-series gas sensor to detect the presence of leaked gas, evaluates the concentration, and triggers protective measures before danger escalates.

In the implementation, the sensor data is processed by a microcontroller, then transmitted to a connected dashboard. This allows users to monitor the environment in real time and take action quickly if needed.

Project Goals

  • Enhance household gas safety
  • Reduce false alarms through calibrated thresholds
  • Provide remote visibility
  • Support automation and prevention

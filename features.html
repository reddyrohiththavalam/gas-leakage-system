System Features

Designed to detect, warn, and protect

Early Detection

The MQ-6 sensor identifies low and high gas concentration levels before they become dangerous.

Alert Systems

Visual indicators and buzzers provide local warnings in the kitchen or room where the leak occurs.

Automated Safety

The controller can switch off the gas supply or activate a ventilation response automatically.

Cloud Dashboard

Live readings are presented in an accessible web dashboard for remote review.

Low Power Design

The prototype is optimized for continued operation with a compact power supply.

Scalable Platform

The design can be extended to larger buildings, laboratories, and industrial kitchens.

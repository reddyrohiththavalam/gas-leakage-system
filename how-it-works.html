How It Works

From sensing to automatic protection

1. Gas Sensing

The gas sensor detects LPG concentration in the surrounding environment and sends an analog signal to the controller.

2. Decision Logic

The microcontroller compares the value against a safe threshold and classifies the situation as normal or hazardous.

3. Response Action

If a leak is confirmed, the device activates a buzzer, triggers a relay, and forwards the alert for remote viewing.

Implementation Flow

The system is designed to operate as a closed loop: detect → evaluate → warn → protect → report. This flow makes the solution suitable for residential kitchens, small restaurants, and other gas-sensitive environments.

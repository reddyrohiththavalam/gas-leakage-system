Project Overview

A practical IoT approach for safer cooking environments

The proposed system combines sensing, control, communication, and monitoring to reduce the risk of gas explosions and fire incidents.

Introduction

Cooking gas is widely used in homes and small businesses, but gas leaks can lead to dangerous accidents if they remain unnoticed. The IoT-based detection system addresses this problem by continuously monitoring gas concentration and activating protective responses before a hazardous event develops.

Objectives

  • Detect LPG leakage at an early stage.
  • Trigger visible and audible warnings immediately.
  • Prevent further gas release by cutting off the supply.
  • Provide remote monitoring through a web dashboard.
Key Features

Everything needed for proactive gas safety

🔔

Alarm Notification

A buzzer and flashing indicator warn users instantly when gas concentration rises above safe levels.

📡

Remote Monitoring

The system publishes data to a dashboard and enables live observation of environmental conditions.

🧰

Automated Control

A relay or solenoid valve can shut off the gas flow automatically during a leak event.
